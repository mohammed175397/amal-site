Healing Guide

Follow these simple steps to feel calmer and more at ease.

  • Take a deep breath: Close your eyes and take slow, deep breaths for a minute.
  • Write your thoughts: Put your feelings into words to release stress.
  • Take a short break: Step away and give yourself a moment to rest.
  • Reach out: Talk to someone you trust or feel safe with.

Take your time. Healing happens step by step.
